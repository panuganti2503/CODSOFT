About

This Article is about Monsoon which is one of the most beautiful season to explorer India, its culture, people, natural beauty and wildlife. The much-awaited monsoon hit the Kerala coast on June 1 and till the end of June or By the first week of July, most parts of country experiences monsoon. Farmers of India welcome monsoon season, It gives them reason to celebrate their festivals, pray for good crops and made special monsoon delicacies. Monsoon in India really gives one of the best natural scenery, as rivers are on full swing, mountains are awake after a long sleep and monsoon decorated the whole valley’s with sparking waterfalls, magnificent lakes, lush green trees and beautiful flowers.
